# Summary

Simple case for a LoPy https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1795343078/lopy-the-lora-wifi-and-bluetooth-iot-development-p

Houses the device when it is on the Pycom expansion carrier to avoid accidental damage 

Screw holes are precut to take a M3 bolt, Holes also provided for antenna and USB connections.

Cover is a loose fit as this is supposed to be a development case designed for quick and tool-less access to the LoPy itself.
